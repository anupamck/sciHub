## Why I saved this

- Hands-on implementation guide for building and improving real-world RAG systems.

## Key points

- Breaks RAG into practical stages: data prep, chunking, retrieval, optional reranking, and generation.
- Explains why hybrid retrieval and reranking often outperform pure vector search in practice.
- Highlights operational concerns such as prompt context construction, cost trade-offs, and output grounding.
