## Why I saved this

- Useful empirical evidence for evaluating long-term software quality trade-offs of AI coding assistants.

## Key points

- Reports productivity gains during initial development with AI assistance (30-55%).
- Finds no significant downstream difference in code evolution time or quality in follow-up tasks.
- Highlights future risk areas such as code bloat and cognitive debt despite neutral maintainability outcomes.
