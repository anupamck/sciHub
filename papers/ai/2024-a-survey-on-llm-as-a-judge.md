## Why I saved this

- Broad reference for designing reliable LLM-based evaluation systems across domains.

## Key points

- Organizes the field around what LLM-as-a-judge is, how to use it, how to improve it, and how to evaluate it.
- Highlights core reliability risks including position/length/style bias and adversarial fragility.
- Reviews practical mitigation strategies spanning prompt design, model tuning, and multi-source aggregation.

## Notes

- Useful companion to empirical judge benchmarks when deciding evaluation methodology.
